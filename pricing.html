Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

Are there any hidden costs in your pricing?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. All costs are clearly outlined in our quotation, including any third-party fees such as certification body charges or testing costs.

What if my project requires more time than estimated?

Our pricing includes reasonable project timelines. If additional time is required due to client-side delays or scope changes, we'll discuss any additional costs upfront before proceeding.

Do you offer discounts for multiple certifications?

Yes, we offer attractive package discounts for clients pursuing multiple certifications simultaneously. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cheques, and online payments. For international clients, we also accept wire transfers and major credit cards through secure payment gateways.

Is there ongoing support after certification?

Yes, all our packages include 3 months of post-certification support. We also offer annual maintenance contracts for ongoing compliance support and certificate renewal assistance.
