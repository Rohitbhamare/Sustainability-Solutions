Who We Are

Sustainability Solutions India (SSI) stands as the premier certification consultancy in India, dedicated to empowering textile chemical companies with world-class sustainability certifications. Since our inception in 2014, we have been at the forefront of the sustainability revolution, helping businesses transform their operations to meet international standards.

Our expertise spans across the entire spectrum of sustainability certifications including GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), ZDHC (Zero Discharge of Hazardous Chemicals), various ISO standards, and REACH compliance. We understand that each certification journey is unique, and we pride ourselves on delivering personalized solutions that align with our clients' specific business objectives and operational constraints.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of both the technical requirements of international standards and the practical challenges faced by Indian manufacturers. Our team combines academic excellence with hands-on industry experience, ensuring that our clients not only achieve certification but also derive tangible business benefits from their sustainability initiatives.

We believe that sustainability is not just about compliance — it's about creating lasting value for businesses, communities, and the environment. This philosophy drives everything we do, from our initial consultations to post-certification support.
