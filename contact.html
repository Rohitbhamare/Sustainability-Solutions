Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How quickly can you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can reach us via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we provide free initial consultations to understand your requirements and provide preliminary guidance on the certification process and timeline.

Can you work with companies outside Mumbai?

We serve clients across India and internationally. We can conduct virtual consultations and provide remote support for most of our services.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

Please have ready: your company details, current certification status, specific standards you're interested in, timeline requirements, and any specific challenges you're facing.

Do you provide services in languages other than English?

Yes, our team can communicate in Hindi, Marathi, and Gujarati in addition to English. We can also arrange for translation services for other languages if needed.
